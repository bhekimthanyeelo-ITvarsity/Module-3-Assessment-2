Methi muthia is a gujarati sncaks . This is very healthy snack and easy to make. Here i have shared steamed and deep fried version. Muthia is one of the famous foods of Gujarat.[1] The name is derived from the way it is made, from the 'gripping' action of the hand. It is a vegan dish. It is made up of chickpea flour, methi (fenugreek), salt, turmeric, chili powder, and an optional bonding agent/sweetener such as sugar and oil
